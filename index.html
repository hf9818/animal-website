Now that you have watched the video, I can tell you a little more about each animal individually.

Dogs

The dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf. Also called the domestic dog, it was selectively bred from an extinct population of wolves during the Late Pleistocene by hunter-gatherers. They live 10-13 years and sleep 12-14 hours a day (adult).

Cats

The cat, also referred to as domestic cat or house cat, is a small domesticated carnivorous mammal. Their lifespan is 13-20 years, and they sleep 12-16 hours a day.

Fish

A fish is an aquatic, anamniotic, gill-bearing vertebrate animal with swimming fins and a hard skull, but lacking limbs with digits.

Hamster

If you live in a tight area like an apartment or a small home, a hamster is the right pet for you. Hamsters are intelligent and can be trained to do simple tricks.

Rabbits

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae. Their lifespan is 9 or 3-5 years, depending on the type. They sleep 6-8 hours daily.

Birds

Birds are warm-blooded vertebrates characterised by feathers, toothless beaked jaws, and a high metabolic rate.

Turtles

Turtles are reptiles with a special shell developed mainly from their ribs. They are divided into two major groups based on how they retract their heads.
